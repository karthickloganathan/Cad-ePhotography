About Me

Karthick was born and raised in Tiruppur, and has been living in Chennai since 2014. A part-time photographer, he uses photography as a way of expressing himself and bringing out the best in his subjects. Through constant research for new techniques and attention to lighting, composition and detail, he is always looking for new and creative ways to bring uniqueness into his photography. He aims to use his images to please as well as question emotions and inspire reaction. His intention is to rifle through the thousands of images I have and find the gems and put them up on this site to share how I see the world, I hope you enjoy.
